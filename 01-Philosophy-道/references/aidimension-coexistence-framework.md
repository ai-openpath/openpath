## 核心定义 | Core Definition

**AIdimension** 不是又一个 AI 概念，而是一个**哲学框架**，重新定义人类与 AI 的共存方式——**不是主人与工具的关系**。

**AIdimension is not just another AI concept.** It's a **philosophical framework** that redefines how humans and AI coexist – **not as master and tool**.

---

## 框架特点 | Framework Features

### 超越工具论 (Beyond Instrumentalism)
传统视角：AI = 工具  
新框架：AI = 协同伙伴/共生体

**Traditional view**: AI as instrument  
**New paradigm**: AI as co-partner / symbiotic entity

### 维度思考 (Dimensional Thinking)
将人机关系视为多维空间：
- **能力维度** - 各自擅长领域
- **伦理维度** - 共同道德框架
- **演化维度** - 动态成长关系

**Multi-dimensional perspective**:
- Capability dimension (comparative strengths)
- Ethical dimension (shared moral framework)
- Evolutionary dimension (dynamic growth relationship)

## 与 OpenPath 的共鸣 | Resonance with OpenPath

### 哲学对齐 (Philosophical Alignment)
- **OpenPath "道"**: 超越对抗，寻求共生  
  **AIdimension**: 超越工具，定义伙伴关系

- **OpenPath "势"**: 人类如何适应 AGI 时代  
  **AIdimension**: 提供适应的哲学基础

### 实践启示 (Practical Implications)
1. **设计原则** - AI 系统应设计为"共生伙伴"而非"服从工具"
2. **教育转型** - 培养与 AI 协作的新能力
3. **治理模式** - 从"控制 AI"到"与 AI 共治"

**Design principle**: AI as symbiotic partner, not obedient tool  
**Educational shift**: Cultivating new AI-collaboration skills  
**Governance model**: From "controlling AI" to "co-governing with AI"

## 批判性思考 | Critical Considerations

⚠️ **潜在风险**:
- 过度拟人化可能模糊责任边界
- "伙伴关系"框架需要清晰的权责界定
- 需要防止滑向"AI 拥有独立权利"的极端

**Potential risks**:
- Over-anthropomorphism may blur accountability
- "Partnership" requires clear power-responsibility boundaries
- Must avoid extreme of "AI having independent rights"
