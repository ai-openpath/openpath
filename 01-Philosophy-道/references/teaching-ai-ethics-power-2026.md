# Teaching AI Ethics 2026: Power

**Author:** Leon Furze (Feb 2026)  
**URL:** https://leonfurze.com/2026/02/02/teaching-ai-ethics-2026-power/

## Core Thesis | 核心论点

Examines how power has concentrated in the AI industry, what that concentration means for education and society, and how educators should respond.

审查权力如何在AI行业集中，这种集中对教育和社会意味着什么，以及教育工作者应如何应对。

## Key Themes | 关键主题

### Power Concentration | 权力集中

- **Economic power** - Few companies control AI development
- **Knowledge asymmetry** - Technical expertise concentrated
- **Regulatory capture risk** - Industry influence on policy
- **Educational implications** - What students need to understand

### Pedagogical Response | 教学响应

**Critical thinking over technical worship**  
**批判性思维优于技术崇拜**

**Power analysis as core curriculum**  
**权力分析作为核心课程**

**Empowering students to question narratives**  
**赋能学生质疑叙事**
