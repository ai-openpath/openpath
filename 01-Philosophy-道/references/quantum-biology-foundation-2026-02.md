## Overview | 概览

量子生物学研究量子力学和理论化学在生物学中的应用，揭示经典生物学无法精确描述的现象。这为理解碳基智能的独特性提供物理基础。

Quantum biology studies applications of quantum mechanics and theoretical chemistry to biology, revealing phenomena that classical biology cannot accurately describe. This provides a physical foundation for understanding the uniqueness of carbon-based intelligence.
