## Core Concept

**Nature-inspired innovation (biomimicry)** is an enthralling domain of AI research where scientists and engineers strive to **emulate the strategies** found in nature.

## Key Insight

### Bidirectional Learning

1. **Nature → AI:** Biomimicry inspires AI design
2. **AI → Nature Understanding:** AI helps us understand nature's strategies better

This creates a **positive feedback loop** between biology and AI.
