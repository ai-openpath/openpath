# Philosophy, Ethics, and the Pursuit of 'Responsible' Artificial Intelligence

**Source:** Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT)  
**URL:** https://www.rit.edu/news/philosophy-ethics-and-pursuit-responsible-artificial-intelligence  
**Date:** 2024  
**Author:** Interview with Prof. Evan Selinger, Department of Philosophy, RIT

## 核心观点 / Core Insights

### 1. 人文视角不可或缺 / Humanities Are Essential

> "AI ethics goes beyond technical fixes. Philosophers and other humanities experts are uniquely skilled to address the nuanced principles, value conflicts, and power dynamics. These skills aren't just crucial for addressing current issues. We desperately need them to foster anticipatory governance."

**中文解读：**
- AI伦理不仅是技术问题，更是价值冲突和权力动态问题
- 哲学家和人文学者具备独特技能：识别价值冲突、预判治理需求
- "预期性治理"（anticipatory governance）：在技术实际造成伤害前识别风险

### 2. 无固定公式，需持续反思 / No Step-by-Step Formula

> "There isn't a step-by-step formula for good governance. In fact, there is a lively debate among experts over how to even define what responsible AI entails."

**关键挑战：**
- ❌ 避免"伦理洗白"（ethics washing）：空洞声明、无约束力的检查清单
- ✅ 建立有意义的问责机制
- ⚖️ 平衡不同立场（与Microsoft等机构的对话）

### 3. 多层次问题域 / Multi-Layer Problem Space

**后端问题：**
- 测试AI聊天机器人的人类成本（human toll from testing）
- 对审核员和红队测试者的心理影响

**政策问题：**
- 面部识别的危险
- 自动化监控的公民自由风险

**企业实践：**
- 负责任的AI审计方法
- 公民自由与安全的平衡

### 4. 与DARPA合作案例 / DARPA Collaboration

**方法论亮点：**
1. **前瞻性风险识别** - 在技术尚未公开前识别伦理问题
2. **设计干预** - 通过改变技术设计来限制风险
3. **跨学科协作** - 哲学家与工程师共同工作

## 教育意义 / Educational Implications

**RIT的AI伦理课程目标：**
- 帮助未来科技领袖思考企业目标
- 理解忽视伦理的社会成本和企业文化成本
- 将伦理关注与人才吸引力联系起来

**关键洞察：**
> "To attract top talent, you need to consider whether your company aligns with their interests and hopes for the future."

伦理不仅是道德义务，也是竞争优势。
