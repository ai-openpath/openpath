### Why Quantum Biology Matters for OpenPath Q2

1. **Photosynthesis Quantum Coherence**
   - Energy transfer at ~100% efficiency via quantum superposition
   - No classical computer can simulate this at molecular scale
   - Proves carbon-based systems have emergent physics beyond AI reach

2. **Avian Navigation**
   - Birds use quantum entanglement in cryptochrome proteins for magnetic sensing
   - 3.8 billion years of evolutionary optimization
   - Demonstrates "embodied quantum cognition" impossible in digital systems

3. **Enzyme Catalysis**
   - Quantum tunneling enables reactions that classical chemistry forbids
   - Foundation of all metabolic pathways
   - Another irreplaceable carbon-based advantage
