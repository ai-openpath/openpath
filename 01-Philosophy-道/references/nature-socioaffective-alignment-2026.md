# Why Human–AI Relationships Need Socioaffective Alignment

**Source**: Nature (2026-02)  
**URL**: https://www.nature.com/articles/s41599-025-04532-5  
**Category**: Human-AI Relationship Theory

## Summary

Academic paper exploring how increasingly capable AI agents generate perception of deeper relationships with users, especially as AI becomes more personalized. Argues for socioaffective alignment between humans and AI systems.

## Key Insights

### Relationship Dynamics
- AI personalization creates illusion of emotional connection
- Users develop genuine feelings toward AI agents
- Need for framework to align AI behavior with human social/emotional needs
