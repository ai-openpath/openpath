## Recent Research & Reviews

### Medical Applications

#### Granzyme B-mimetic Nanozyme (Nature Communications)
- **Authors:** Xueyan Hu, Qiqi Liu, Xinglu Huang
- **Innovation:** Pd-FTn nanozyme mimicking cytotoxic T lymphocyte function
- **Mechanism:** Binuclear catalytic center in functionalized nanovesicles
- **Application:** Targeted cancer therapy via caspase-dependent apoptosis
- **Biomimetic Principle:** T cell-inspired anticancer immunity

#### Light-controlled Synthetic Communication Networks (Nature Communications)
- **Authors:** Ahmed Z. Sihorwala, Alexander J. Lin, Brian Belardi
- **Innovation:** Engineered connexin nanopores with light control
- **Function:** Chemical signal exchange between synthetic cells
- **Achievement:** Programmable communication networks mimicking cellular interactions
- **Biomimetic Principle:** Intercellular communication via connexon channels

### Biohybrid Systems

#### Hiveopolis: Reimagining the Beehive (Nature Reviews Materials)
- **Authors:** Asya Ilgün, Thomas Schmickl
- **Concept:** Beehives as biohybrid superorganisms
- **Components:** 
  - Living fungal materials
  - Digital technologies
  - Honeybee colonies
- **Approach:** Transdisciplinary multispecies resilience
- **Innovation:** Bio-inspired algorithms negotiating material costs, time, energy, structural performance
- **Philosophy:** Sustainable co-habitation through adaptive architecture

#### Biohybrid Neuromuscular Robots (Nature Reviews Electrical Engineering)
- **Author:** Caroline Beyer
- **Source:** Science Robotics article review
- **Innovation:** Wireless bioelectronic robot integrating:
  - Living motor neurons
  - Cardiomyocytes for movement power
- **Significance:** Crossing biological-mechanical boundary

### Navigation & Autonomy

#### Bio-inspired Navigation Systems (Nature Reviews Electrical Engineering)
- **Authors:** Chandan Sheikder, Weimin Zhang, Yichang Liu
- **Application:** GPS-denied environment navigation
  - Disaster zones
  - Space exploration
  - Deep ocean operations
- **Methods:**
  - Bio-inspired path integration
  - Neural mapping
- **Outcome:** Autonomous robot navigation without external positioning

### Materials Science

#### Polar Bear-inspired Aerogel Fibers (Nature Reviews Materials)
- **Author:** Ariane Vartanian
- **Source:** Science report review
- **Innovation:** Stretchy aerogel fiber sweater
- **Structure:** Core-shell design inspired by polar bear fur
- **Properties:** Exceptionally warm yet thin
- **Biomimetic Principle:** Structural thermal insulation

### Biotechnology

#### Lasso-grafted Designer Cytokines (Nature Biomedical Engineering)
- **Authors:** Aphrodite Kapurniotu, Jürgen Bernhagen
- **Innovation:** Macrocyclic peptide grafting into Fc regions
- **Benefits:**
  - Longer circulation half-lives
  - Enhanced blood-brain barrier penetration
- **Application:** Cytokine receptor agonist therapeutics
- **Biomimetic Principle:** Engineered protein stability
