## 核心观点 | Key Points

Bernard Marr 总结 2026 年 AI 伦理的 8 大趋势，聚焦**信任与问责**：

Bernard Marr summarizes 8 AI ethics trends for 2026, focusing on **trust and accountability**:

1. **透明度要求升级** | Transparency requirements escalate
2. **算法偏见治理** | Algorithmic bias governance
3. **隐私保护强化** | Privacy protection strengthened
4. **可解释性成为基线** | Explainability becomes baseline
5. **跨国监管协调** | Cross-border regulatory coordination
6. **企业伦理委员会普及** | Corporate ethics committees widespread
7. **用户权利法典化** | User rights codified
8. **AI 审计标准化** | AI audit standardization
