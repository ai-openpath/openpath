## Framework Overview | 框架概述

New incident response framework specifically designed for AI systems, documenting:
- Real-world attack case studies
- Defense mechanisms for AI-specific threats
- Response protocols adapted for intelligent systems

专为AI系统设计的新事件响应框架，记录：
- 真实世界的攻击案例研究

- 为智能系统调整的响应协议

1. **Detection** - Identifying AI-specific attack patterns
2. **Containment** - Isolating compromised AI components
3. **Analysis** - Understanding attack vectors unique to AI
4. **Recovery** - Restoring AI system integrity
5. **Prevention** - Hardening against future attacks
