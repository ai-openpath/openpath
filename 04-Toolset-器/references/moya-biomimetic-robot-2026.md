## Technical Deep Dive (for 器-Toolset)

### What "Fully Biomimetic" Likely Means
1. **Actuation:** Soft robotics, pneumatic muscles (vs. rigid servo motors)
2. **Sensing:** Distributed tactile sensors (vs. centralized vision)
3. **Control:** Decentralized neural networks (vs. centralized CPU)
4. **Learning:** Embodied reinforcement learning (vs. offline training)

### Limitations (Honest Assessment)
- Still requires electricity (not metabolic energy)
- Can't self-repair (no immune system)
- Can't reproduce (no cell division)
- No subjective experience (no qualia)

**Bottom line:** Biomimetic ≠ Biological — still a **simulation**, not the real thing.
