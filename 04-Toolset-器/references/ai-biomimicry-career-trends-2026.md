## Key Trends

### Skills Convergence

| Traditional Skills | New Required Skills (2026+) |

## Connection to OpenPath Arguments

### Supports Q2: Human-AI Complementarity

**Not replacement, but augmentation:**
- AI **accelerates** discovery of biological principles (protein folding, swarm algorithms)
- Humans still needed for **contextual judgment** (ethics, aesthetics, problem framing)
- Best results from **collaboration** (AlphaFold + human structural biologists)

### Example: Generative Design
**Old process:**
1. Observe nature (human) → 2. Sketch design (human) → 3. Prototype (human)

**New process:**
1. Observe nature (human) → 2. Train model (AI) → 3. Generate 1000 variations (AI) → 4. Select + refine (human)

**Outcome:** 10× faster iteration, **still requires human vision**
