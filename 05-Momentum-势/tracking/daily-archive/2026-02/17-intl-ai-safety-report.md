## 核心发现 | Key Findings

### 事件举报障碍 (Incident Reporting Barrier)
> **报告原文**: "Embarrassment or fear of further harm can make individuals and institutions reluctant to report incidents of AI-enabled fraud or abuse."

**尴尬感或对二次伤害的恐惧**，使个人和机构不愿举报 AI 欺诈或滥用事件。

**Critical insight**: Fear and shame create **systemic underreporting** of AI harms.
