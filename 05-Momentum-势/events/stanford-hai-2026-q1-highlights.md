## Research Highlights

### Evaluation & Benchmarking
- **Feb 02, 2026**: "Smart Enough to Do Math, Dumb Enough to Fail: The Hunt for a Better AI Test"
  - HAI workshop on new evaluation methods
  - Focus: Hidden capabilities vs. test-taking performance
  - Challenge to current benchmarking approaches

- **Jan 26, 2026**: "AI Can't Do Physics Well – And That's a Roadblock to Autonomy"
  - QuantiPhy benchmark introduced
  - Tests numerical reasoning about physical properties (size, speed, distance)
  - Current models struggle with basic physical estimates
  - Includes training framework for improvement

### Neuroscience & AI
- **Jan 21, 2026**: "AI Reveals How Brain Activity Unfolds Over Time"
  - Deep learning model transforming brain data into trajectories
  - Applications: Understanding thought, emotion, neurological disease

### Enterprise AI Ethics
- **Jan 13, 2026**: "Translating Centralized AI Principles Into Localized Practice"

### Clinical AI Caution
- **Jan 07, 2026**: "Why 'Zero-Shot' Clinical Predictions Are Risky"
  - Authors: Suhana Bedi, Jason Alan Fries, Nigam H. Shah
  - Warning: Models generate plausible timelines from patterns
  - Need: Calibration and auditing before clinical deployment
