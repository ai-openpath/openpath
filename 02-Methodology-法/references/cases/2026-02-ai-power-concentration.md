## 核心论断

> "AI discourse has become thoroughly hegemonic. The idea that AI is 'inevitable,' that schools 'must' integrate AI tools, that workers 'will' be replaced unless they 'adapt,' that nations 'will fall behind' unless they embrace AI development: **these are not neutral observations. They are ideological positions** that serve specific interests, primarily the interests of the companies selling AI products and services."

## 霸权理论（Gramsci）

### 什么是霸权（Hegemony）？

> "Hegemony refers to the dominance of one group over others in society, **not through force but through the establishment of norms, beliefs, and values** that come to seem natural or inevitable. Hegemonic power operates most effectively when it becomes invisible, when the status quo appears to be the only possible state of affairs."

### AI霸权的表现

**话语控制：**
- "AI不可避免"（inevitable）
- "学校必须集成AI工具"（must）
- "工人必须适应否则被淘汰"（will be replaced）
- "国家必须拥抱AI否则落后"（will fall behind）

**这些不是中性观察，而是意识形态立场**

## 伦理问题的互联关系

作者在2023年文章中提出：所有AI伦理问题都通过**权力**关系互联

| 伦理问题 | 与权力的关系 |
|---------|-------------|
| **偏见（Bias）** | 反映谁的世界观主导训练数据 |
| **环境（Environment）** | 影响不成比例地落在边缘社区 |
| **隐私（Privacy）** | 侵蚀使监控和控制成为可能 |
| **劳动（Labour）** | 从全球南方工人提取价值 |

**2026年变化：权力集中加剧，挑战这些实践变得更困难**

> "When a handful of companies control the infrastructure, the talent, the data, and increasingly the policy frameworks that govern AI, challenging their practices requires challenging concentrations of wealth and influence that rival nation-states."

## 教育者面临的困境

### 问题

> "Is it possible for AI to be developed ethically under conditions of extreme power concentration?"

### 教育场景的意识形态压力

- 学校被迫"必须集成AI"
- 教师需"适应AI时代"
- 学生要"为AI就业市场准备"

**这些话语本身就是霸权操作**

## 对OpenPath的启示

### 1. 升维共情的反霸权策略

**识别霸权话语：**
- "AI不可避免"→ 质疑：谁说的？为什么？
- "必须适应"→ 反问：谁定义"适应"？谁受益？

**反制方法（传播学角度）：**
- 揭露话语背后的利益集团
- 提供替代叙事（alternative narratives）
- 强调人类主动性（human agency）
