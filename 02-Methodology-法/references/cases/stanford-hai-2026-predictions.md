## 核心预测

### AI影响评估框架（重点）

**引用原文**:
> "This year I expect we'll start developing ways to evaluate the impact of an AI system, its technical features, its training population, how to..."

**关键要素**:
1. **系统影响评估** (evaluate the impact)
2. **技术特性分析** (technical features)
3. **训练数据人群** (training population)
4. **评估方法开发** (develop ways to evaluate)
