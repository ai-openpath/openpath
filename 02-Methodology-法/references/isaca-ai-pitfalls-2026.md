# Avoiding AI Pitfalls in 2026: Lessons from Top 2025 Incidents

**Source:** ISACA Now Blog (2025)  
**URL:** https://www.isaca.org/resources/news-and-trends/isaca-now-blog/2025/avoiding-ai-pitfalls-in-2026-lessons-learned-from-top-2025-incidents

## Key Insight | 核心见解

> "In 2025, many organizations discovered that AI incidents slipped into everyday operations and created problems where few were looking."

**2025年，许多组织发现AI事件潜入日常运营，在少有人关注的地方制造问题。**

## Common Pitfalls Identified | 识别的常见陷阱

1. **Hidden operational risks** - AI issues in blind spots
2. **Lack of monitoring** - No visibility into AI decision-making
3. **Inadequate incident response** - Unprepared for AI-specific failures
4. **Governance gaps** - Missing accountability structures

## Lessons for 2026 | 对2026年的教训

**Proactive monitoring:** Don't wait for visible failures  
**主动监控：** 不要等待明显的失败

**End-to-end visibility:** Track AI throughout operations  
**端到端可见性：** 在整个运营中跟踪AI

**Incident preparedness:** AI-specific response protocols  
**事件准备：** 针对AI的特定响应协议
